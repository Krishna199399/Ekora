Risk Avoidance

Avoid Common Mistakes That Delay Growth

Many cosmetic projects face delays or instability due to structural planning oversights. Identifying these risks early makes development more efficient.

✕

Poor Market Validation

Launching products without clarifying target positioning or verifying shelf appeal against consumer expectations.

✕

Weak Scalability

Formulations developed in small laboratory beakers that fail when processed in large factory vessels.

✕

Manufacturing Issues

Selecting contract manufacturers without validating their GMP certifications, stability testing, or QMS reliability.

✕

Regulatory Misunderstandings

Overlooking registration procedures, dossier filings, and packaging text validation, leading to costly launch delays.

✕

Supplier Dependency

Relying on rare or single-sourced ingredients that cause inventory gaps when production demand spikes.

✕

Product Stability Problems

Skipping container-compatibility or real-time shelf checks, resulting in emulsion separation or container deformation.
